National museum of Valjevo

The Valjevo National Museum was founded in 1951 . Today, its central building is located in the center of Valjevo , on the banks of the river Kolubara , not far from Tesnjar , the old city bazaar, the new bazaar, Knez Miloseva Street and the Grand Hotel.

National Museum of Pancevo

The building of the Magistrate in Pancevo is located on King Peter I Square no. 7, it was built in the period from 1833 until 1838. The building is under state protection as a cultural monument of great importance The building was built according to the plans of the border builder Major Hyman.

Museum of Yugoslavia

The Museum of Yugoslavia is a museum institution that collects, studies and presents various objects related to the history of Yugoslavia . It was founded in 1996.
